Where & when did Web App originate?
Internet business first started in 1999 when Web App, our founder, registered a company in Hong Kong to sell websites. Web App was incorporated in the Labuan Financial Services Centre, Malaysia on October 20, 2004.

Currently, Web App is incorporated in Hong Kong and has its headquarters there. Web App is also incorporated as an international company in the UAE (Dubai) with a bank account in Dubai and conducts its business world-wide via the Internet. Customers purchase services/products directly from Web App. Purchasing online Website Builder & hosting services/products and receiving these services online are definitely legal. When a customer promotes and secures sales for Web App, he earns a commission from such action. It is the same as word-of-mouth advertising to sell company’s products. The services/products are fairly priced compared to those of the same specifications and quality sold by other competitor companies. There is no additional cost for participation in the Web App Customer Reward Program. The commission we pay to our customers is for their service in securing the sales. What is CORS?
CORS, aka Cross-Origin Resource Sharing, is a mechanism that enables many resources (e.g. images, stylesheets, scripts, fonts) on a web page to be requested from another domain outside the domain from which the resource originated.
